Question Palette Legend

  • Green (Hexagon): You have answered the question
  • Red (Hexagon): You have not answered the question
  • Purple (Circle): You have marked the question for review
  • White (Square): You have not visited the question yet
  • Blue Border: Currently viewing this question

How to Attempt Questions

  • Click on any option (A, B, C, or D) to select your answer
  • Click "Save & Next" to save answer and move to next question
  • Click "Clear Response" to deselect your answer
  • Click "Mark for Review & Next" to mark question for later review
  • You can change your answer anytime before final submission
IMPORTANT: Make sure to click "Save & Next" after selecting an answer. Simply clicking on an option without saving will NOT record your response.
PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES:

  • Use updated version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge browser

  • Minimum screen resolution: 1024x768
