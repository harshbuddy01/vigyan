Privacy Policy

Last updated December 25, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to IIN. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide when you register, including:

  • Name — To personalize your experience
  • Email — For communications and account management
  • Phone — For important updates and support
  • Payment Info — Processed securely by payment partners
  • Test Data — To track progress and provide insights

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and manage access to test series
  • Send login details and test results
  • Process payments securely
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Sharing Information

We do not sell your data. We only share with service providers necessary for operations, including payment processors, email services, analytics providers, and legal authorities when required.

Data Security

We implement security measures including SSL encryption, secure database storage, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, object to processing, request data portability, and withdraw consent. Contact us to exercise these rights.
